[ About 03 // PERSPECTIVE ]

Brand & Product.
Same problem.

UX & Decisions

Most of my interest in UX comes from watching how people make decisions when the information isn't complete. How they navigate uncertainty. That's more interesting than interface polish.

Trust & Transition

Now I work across brand and product because I think they're the same problem. A packaging decision and a checkout flow are both answering the same question: does this person trust us enough to take the next step? I'm most useful in the room where those two conversations happen at the same time.
